Our Cocktails

At Routt Distillery, our cocktails are crafted to showcase the unique profiles of our hand-distilled spirits. From refreshing gin concoctions to robust whiskey blends, each recipe highlights the flavors of Colorado-grown grains and our innovative approach to distilling. Enjoy these creations at our Steamboat Springs tasting room or mix them up at home!

Summer Gin Cocktails

Bright and breezy, our Summer Gin dances with citrus in cocktails that capture the essence of a Steamboat Springs summer. The Gimlet is a must-try—its zesty simplicity is irresistible.

Gimlet

  • 2 oz Summer Gin
  • 3/4 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Gin & Tonic

  • 2 oz Summer Gin
  • 4 oz tonic water (adjust to taste)

Fill a highball glass with ice, pour in the gin, and top with tonic water. Stir gently and garnish with a citrus slice of your choice. Play around with different tonics and ratios—we like the 1:2 gin-to-tonic ratio.

Winter Gin Cocktails

Cozy up with our Winter Gin, where warming spices meet a rare harmony with bitters. The Fitzgerald, an overlooked classic, finds its perfect match here—our version cuts the sugar for a balanced sip that other gins can’t pull off. It’s one of our absolute favorites.

Fitzgerald

  • 2 oz Winter Gin
  • 1/2 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters

Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a chilled coupe glass.

Martini

  • 2 oz Winter Gin
  • 0.5 oz dry vermouth (or 1 oz Dolin Blanc vermouth for a richer twist)

Stir gin and vermouth with ice in a mixing glass until well-chilled. Strain into a chilled martini glass and garnish with a lemon twist or olive.

London Dry Gin Cocktails

Our London Dry Gin shines in classic cocktails with its bold, juniper-forward profile. The dry Martini keeps it crisp, while the gin-heavy Negroni lets the botanicals take center stage.

Martini

  • 2 oz London Dry Gin
  • 1/2 oz dry vermouth

Stir gin and vermouth with ice in a mixing glass until well-chilled. Strain into a chilled martini glass and garnish with a lemon twist or olive.

Negroni

  • 1.5 oz London Dry Gin
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth

Stir all ingredients with ice in a mixing glass until well-chilled. Strain into a rocks glass over a large ice cube and garnish with an orange twist.

Sarvis Gin Cocktails

Our Sarvis Gin is delicious neat over a big ice cube, but the Sarvis Gin Fizz brings the berry flavors to the forefront with carbonation and acidity.

Neat

  • 2 oz Sarvis Gin

Pour over a large ice cube in a rocks glass and enjoy.

Sarvis Gin Fizz

  • 2 oz Sarvis Gin
  • 3/4 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 oz simple syrup
  • 3 oz sparkling water

Build in a highball glass with ice: pour gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup, then top with sparkling water. Stir vertically to mix and serve.

Vodka Cocktails

This is a Vodka you can sip cold—we didn’t say shoot, we said sip. The classic Vodka Highball is a great way to enjoy its crisp profile.

Neat

  • 2 oz Vodka (chilled)

Pour into a chilled glass and sip slowly to enjoy its smooth, clean finish.

Vodka Highball

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 6 oz very bubbly, cold sparkling water
  • Orange peel (for garnish)

Build in a chilled highball glass with large ice cubes: pour vodka, top with very bubbly, cold sparkling water, and stir vertically to mix. Garnish with an orange peel.

Emerald Single Malt Highball

Pretty dang good served neat, but also works fantastic in a Japanese Whiskey Highball. The ratio is key—too much sparkling water makes it whiskey-flavored water, too little and it’s just watered-down whiskey. We like 1 part Emerald Mountain Single Malt Whiskey to 3 parts sparkling water but experiment from 1:2 to 1:4. Think this is crazy for whiskey and water? Search Youtube for Japanese Highball cocktail...

Japanese Whiskey Highball

  • 2 oz Emerald Mountain Single Malt Whiskey
  • 6 oz very bubbly, cold sparkling water
  • Citrus peel (for garnish)

Build in a chilled highball glass with large ice cubes: pour whiskey, top with very bubbly, cold sparkling water, and stir vertically to mix. Garnish with a citrus peel of your choice. Cold is key—use the coldest glass and water you can get.

Storm Mountain Old Fashioned

Old Fashioned, baby! The chocolate malt in our Storm Mountain Single Malt Whiskey pairs perfectly with the bitters in this classic cocktail.

Old Fashioned

  • 2 oz Storm Mountain Single Malt Whiskey
  • 1/4 oz simple syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange twist (for garnish)

In a mixing glass, combine simple syrup and bitters. Add whiskey and ice, then stir until well-chilled. Strain over a large ice cube in a rocks glass and garnish with an orange twist.
