Cookie Policy

Introduction

At Routt Distillery, we value your privacy. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website (routtdistillery.com). By using our site, you consent to the use of these technologies as described below.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. We primarily use local storage, a similar technology, to enhance your experience. These tools help us remember your preferences and improve site functionality.

How We Use Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies and local storage for the following purposes:

  • Essential Functionality: We store your preference to dismiss the age verification banner and cookie consent banner using local storage. This ensures you don’t see these prompts repeatedly.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies for analytics, advertising, or tracking your behavior across other sites.

Your Choices

You can manage how we use these technologies:

  • Cookie Banner: On your first visit, you’ll see a banner allowing you to accept all, accept necessary, or decline cookies. Since we only use local storage for basic functionality, declining has no significant impact.
  • Browser Settings: You can clear local storage or disable it in your browser settings, though this may reset your preferences on our site.
