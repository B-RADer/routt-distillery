Welcome to Routt Distillery

We make our spirits right here in Steamboat Springs - crafted by our own hands with Colorado-grown grains. Not outsourced. Not from another state. Just local, honest whiskey, gin, and vodka. As a family-owned distillery, we pour our heart into every bottle, creating delicious cocktails that shine in our tasting room against the backdrop of Steamboat’s beautiful mountain views.

Open Friday & Saturday 1-6 pm

Spirits

At Routt Distillery, we distill every spirit from scratch in Steamboat Springs using only the finest Colorado-grown grains. Our whiskeys offer rich, layered flavors, our gins bring a crisp, balanced bite with carefully selected botanicals, and our vodka is smooth enough to savor slowly. Each bottle reflects our hands-on dedication to crafting quality spirits that stand out.

Cocktails

Our cocktails are where creativity meets craftsmanship. Designed to highlight the unique profiles of our spirits, these drinks blend local flair with a twist of family ingenuity. From refreshing gin fizzes that capture summer in a glass to robust whiskey highballs that warm you up on a snowy Steamboat night, every sip is an invitation to taste the spirit of Colorado.

About Us

Born in 2022, Routt Distillery is more than just a business—it’s our family’s passion project. Rooted in the rugged beauty of Colorado’s landscape, we set out to create spirits that reflect the land we love. With every batch, we blend tradition with innovation, crafting small-batch whiskey, gin, and vodka that tell a story of care, community, and the mountains that inspire us.

Find Us

Join us at our Steamboat Springs tasting room, open Friday and Saturday from 1-6 pm. We’re a short drive from downtown, offering a cozy spot to enjoy our spirits and cocktails with a view. Call us at 970.367.7477 or check out our Google Maps page for directions and reviews. Follow us online for updates and events—we’d love to share a drink with you!
