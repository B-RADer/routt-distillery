About Us

Nestled in Steamboat Springs, Colorado, Routt Distillery is a small, family-run operation with a big passion for crafting unique local spirits that embody the rugged spirit of the Rocky Mountains. We work closely with our farmers and maltsters to source the finest Colorado-grown grains, ensuring every bottle reflects the land we love. After distilling, our spent grain goes to Hayden Fresh Farm, where it feeds their pigs and chickens, supporting local agriculture and sustainability. Each sip tells a story of quality, creativity, and our deep connection to the community.

A Family Affair

Family is the heartbeat of Routt Distillery. We’re a close-knit crew, united by a shared love for crafting spirits that reflect our Colorado roots. From distilling to bottling, every step is a hands-on family effort, infused with laughter, hard work, and pride. We can’t wait to share our passion—and our spirits—with you.

Meet the Distiller

I’m Brad, the owner and distiller at Routt Distillery, where I channel my lifelong love for creating into every bottle we produce. With a Masters in Food Studies from NYU and a PhD in Economic Geography from UC Davis—where I dove deep into the wine industry and high-value agricultural regions—I bring a unique perspective to distilling. My journey began at Davis with my first distilling course, igniting a passion that burns brighter with every batch. Making unique products and sharing them with others is what I love to do. From our small-batch whiskeys to our one-of-a-kind gins, I pour my heart into every drop, delivering flavors that stand out and tell our story.

Supply Chain Guru

Libby Christensen, the Chief Supply Chain Logistics Specialist at Routt Distillery, is responsible for turning relationships into sources of raw ingredients that are the foundation of every bottle we produce. Whether sourcing unique grains, connecting with malters and farmers, or personally hauling the goods to keep things moving, she makes sure we have what we need to craft spirits that stand out. With a knack for tracking down the best ingredients and securing grants to fuel our creativity, Libby keeps the distillery running smoothly.

Design Maestro

Dan Lemmer, our head graphic designer and my brother-in-law, brings the Routt Distillery brand to life with his creative vision. From designing eye-catching labels to crafting our distinctive logo, Dan’s artistic talent ensures every bottle reflects the spirit of our Colorado roots. His work blends creativity with precision, making our spirits stand out on the shelf as much as they do in the glass.

Our Little Helpers

Our kids, ages 5 and 8, are the youngest members of the Routt Distillery crew. They bring boundless energy and curiosity to our family-friendly distillery, where having them around reminds us to always be the heroes they see us as. Their presence inspires us to act responsibly, crafting spirits with care for our community and the next generation.

Our Distillery Dogs

Oban and Jameson, our distillery dogs named after fine whiskeys, are beloved fixtures at Routt. Oban passed away last year, but his memory lives on in the stories we tell. Jameson, our resident lounge expert, can often be found sprawled across the tasting room couch, ready to keep you company with a wagging tail and a friendly nudge.
