Our Spirits

At Routt Distillery, we craft our spirits with a passion for flavor and innovation, using the finest Colorado-grown grains to create bottles that reflect the rugged beauty of Steamboat Springs. Our small-batch creations are designed to be unique, showcasing the best of Colorado agriculture. From crisp vodkas to complex whiskeys—and gins that inspire distinct cocktails—each spirit elevates your sipping experience.

Emerald Mountain Single Malt Whiskey

Aged in new and used oak barrels, our Emerald Mountain Single Malt Whiskey boasts a rich, malty depth with notes of caramel, honey, and a smooth, lingering finish. Savor it neat to appreciate its complexity or mix it into a Japanese Highball.

Storm Mountain Single Malt Whiskey

Inspired by the rugged Storm Mountain (aka Mt. Werner), this single malt is crafted with chocolate malt in the mash bill, delivering deep flavors with hints of cocoa, toasted oak, and a touch of spice. Best enjoyed by a crackling fire or in an Old Fashioned.

Bourbon

Still resting in barrels, our Bourbon with a 20% wheat mash bill promises a soft profile with notes of caramel, vanilla, and a smooth lingering finish. Check back soon for its release!

Storm Mountain Bourbon

Also aging in barrels, this bold bourbon with chocolate malt in the mash bill will offer rich cocoa, warm spice, and oak undertones. Stay tuned for its debut!

Summer Gin

Inspired by Steamboat Springs summers, this light and floral gin bursts with citrus character and a subtle spice from pink peppercorns. Perfect for a Gimlet or gin and tonic.

Winter Gin

Capturing a Steamboat winter, this gin balances juniper with bitter orange, cardamom, and nutmeg, finishing with a warm glow. Ideal for a Fitzgerald or martini.

London Dry Gin

A bold, juniper-forward take on the classic style, this gin bursts with botanicals, balanced by citrus and spice. Great for the classics like a Martini and a Negroni.

Sarvisberry Flavored Gin

Infused with hand-picked Colorado sarvisberries soaked in Summer Gin for 2 months, this gin offers a fruity twist with subtle tartness and no added sugar—just berry sweetness. Try it in a Fizz or over ice. We only make around 100 bottles of this each year so get it when you can!

Vodka

Distilled from Colorado-grown grains, our premium vodka offers a silky-smooth texture with a clean, crisp finish. Gone are the days of burning, foul tasting vodka. You can tell this Vodka is made with love and care from Colorado Grain. Sip it chilled or mix it into a Highball.
